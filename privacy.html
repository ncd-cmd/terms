Privacy Policy

Paddock Pass  ·  Effective Date: March 31, 2026  ·  Operated by Nicholas Daniele

1. Introduction

We operate the Paddock Pass mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and username when you register.
  • Game activity: Team selections, chip activations, transfer history, and league participation.
  • League content: League names, icons, and settings you create.
  • Authentication tokens: Session identifiers used to verify your identity. Stored locally on your device and not used for tracking or advertising.
  • Basic device information: Device type, OS version, and app version for debugging purposes.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Advertising identifiers (no ads, no ad-tracking SDKs)
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking data

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate the service: Manage your account, calculate scores, run leagues, and display standings.
  • Authenticate you: Verify your identity on each request.
  • Improve the App: Diagnose issues and fix bugs.
  • Enforce our Terms: Detect and prevent misuse or unauthorized access.
We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioral targeting.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Infrastructure: Your data is stored on US-based servers. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication (Google) handles identity management. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Access: Only the App's backend services access your data. Administrative access is limited to service operators.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.
  • With other users: Your username, team name, scores, and league standings are visible to other league members. Your email is never shown to other users.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase Authentication (Google) for identity management and US-based infrastructure providers for hosting. We share only the minimum data necessary to operate the service.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or governmental request.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, your user record and all personally identifiable information are permanently removed. Retained game data (scores, standings) is anonymized.

8. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. Account deletion is available in-app via Settings > Delete Account. To exercise any other rights, contact us at the email below.

We do not sell or share your personal information. To submit a data request, contact support@chaunga.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. By creating an account, users confirm they are at least 13 years of age. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete that account promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above and notify you within the App. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

Email: support@chaunga.com

Last updated: March 31, 2026.

Privacy Policy

Fantasy One  ·  Effective Date: March 31, 2026  ·  Operated by Nicholas Daniele, Rhode Island, United States

1. Introduction

We operate the Fantasy One mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and username when you register.
  • Authentication credentials: Managed by Firebase Authentication (operated by Google) or Apple Sign-In. We do not store your password directly.
  • Terms of Service acceptance: A timestamp and version identifier recording when you accepted our Terms.
  • Game activity: Team selections (drivers, constructor, powertrain supplier), chip activations, transfer history, and league participation.
  • League content: League names, icons, and settings you create.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Authentication tokens: Firebase Authentication tokens and session identifiers used to verify your identity on each request. These are stored locally on your device and are not used for tracking or advertising.
  • Timestamps: When your account was created, when you accepted the Terms of Service, and when game actions occurred (e.g., transfers, chip activations, scoring events).
  • Basic device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version are collected for debugging and service improvement.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise or coarse location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Advertising identifiers (we do not display ads or use ad-tracking SDKs)
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate the service: Create and manage your account, build teams, calculate scores, run leagues, and display standings.
  • Authenticate you: Verify your identity on each request using Firebase Authentication tokens.
  • Communicate with you: Send service-related notifications (e.g., scoring updates, league invitations). We do not send marketing emails.
  • Maintain and improve the App: Diagnose technical issues, fix bugs, and improve the service.
  • Enforce our Terms: Detect and prevent misuse, abuse, or unauthorized access.
We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioral targeting.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Infrastructure: Your data is stored on managed PostgreSQL databases hosted by DigitalOcean in the United States. Cached data uses managed Redis, also on DigitalOcean.
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication (operated by Google) handles identity and credential management. Google's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Database connections use encrypted channels.
  • Access: Only the App's backend services access your data programmatically. Administrative access is protected by secret keys and is limited to service operators.
  • Data processing agreements: We have data processing agreements in place with our infrastructure providers (DigitalOcean, Google/Firebase) as required by applicable data protection law.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Your username, team name, scores, and league standings are visible to other members of your leagues. Your email address is never shown to other users.
  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for identity and login management. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • DigitalOcean — for server hosting, database, and cache infrastructure. Subject to DigitalOcean's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenF1 API — for Formula 1 race data. We send no user data to OpenF1.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account through the App, your user record and all personally identifiable information are permanently removed. Your teams are deactivated and any retained game data (historical scores, standings) is anonymized — no information that could identify you remains.
  • Anonymized game data: De-identified game data (scores, standings) from which no individual can be re-identified may be retained indefinitely for service operation.

7. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law, including within 72 hours where required by GDPR.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Delete your account and associated personal data (available in-app via Settings > Delete Account).
  • Export your data in a portable format (contact us to request this).
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account.

8.1 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, request deletion of your personal information, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information.

We do not sell or share your personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@fantasyoneapp.com.

8.2 European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your data is contract performance (processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interest (maintaining security, preventing abuse, and improving the service).

You have additional rights including data portability, restriction of processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Contact us at the email below with any concerns.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. By creating an account, users confirm that they are at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete that account and associated data promptly.

10. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above and notify you within the App. Material changes will be communicated prominently before they take effect. Continued use after a policy update constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, contact us at:

Email: support@fantasyoneapp.com
Location: Rhode Island, United States
